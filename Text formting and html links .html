LATUR CITY



Latur is a major commercial and educational hub in the Marathwada region of Maharashtra, India. Formerly known as Lattalut, it served as the ancestral base of the ancient Rashtrakuta dynasty. Today, it is celebrated for its historic caves, vibrant sugar industries, and growing cosmopolitan culture.



LATUR BASIC FACTS

  • State - MAHARASTRA
  • District - Latur (District Headquaters)
  • Population - over 5,00,000 People
  • Main Economy - Agriculture, trade, education, and small industries

Famous for the "Latur Pattern" of education, which became well known for producing strong board-exam results.
